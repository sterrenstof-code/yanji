For my own website I wanted to show the simplicity and elegance that Sterrenstof stands for, whilst still showcasing the fun and unique capabilites of custom webdesign. Veryas Child is a beauty company established in 2020 by Johanna and Edna Kleine-Birkenheuer, and is headquartered in Fleckenberg, Germany. They formulate skin, hair and body care products of the finest quality, sourced on plant-based and laboratory-made ingredients.

For this project I used HTML5, CSS3 and JS6. Most of the website is made with CSS, although JS is responsible for the viewport effects, the header changing color upon scroll, and the ´all´ section, which is a client side application that uses .filter and .map to manipulate the DOM. At the moment I am rebuilding this website into a working e-commerce site, with REST API´s and Node.js.
